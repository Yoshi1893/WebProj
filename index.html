Our Story

A Garden Sanctuary for Life's Finest Celebrations

Nestled in the heart of the city, 9 Waves Events Place is where nature meets elegance. Our lush, wave-inspired gardens and versatile event spaces create the perfect backdrop for weddings, debuts, corporate gatherings, and intimate celebrations.

We believe every event should feel like a journey — one that flows naturally, like waves, from one beautiful moment to the next.
